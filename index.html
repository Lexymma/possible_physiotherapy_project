About Us

Possible Physiotherapy is a small company of Chartered Physiotherapists with many years of experience delivering clinical care in the community. All staff are fully insured, registered with the Health and Care Professions Council and are DBS cleared. We have extensive experience in working with older people, including those with dementia and enjoy finding new ways to engage people in activity and exercise to continue to be independent in their own home. A key feature of Possible Therapists is to be a patient advocate and work in partnership with the person and their friends and family so they can be as independent as possible. We provide a specialist multi conditions community physiotherapy and rehabilitation service for people in their own home, care home or any residential setting. We visit clients across Hampshire.

What we do

  • Neurorehabilitation for conditions like Parkinson’s Disease and Multiple Sclerosis. We can help to restore your confidence and make movement easier.
  • We see people at every different stage of recovery after a stroke or diagnosis of a neurological condition.
  • Post critical care or long-term hospital stay.
  • Pulmonary rehabilitation.
  • Rehabilitation & exercise for people living with and beyond cancer.
  • Vestibular rehabilitation (treatment of balance and dizziness).
  • General rehabilitation following a period of illness or operation.
  • Pre and post operative orthopaedic surgery.
